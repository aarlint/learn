⏱️ Elapsed Time

Elapsed time is how much time passes between a start time and an end time.

Example: If you start reading at 2:00 and stop at 2:45, the elapsed time is 45 minutes.

Tip: Count by 5s on the clock, or subtract the times!

🛒 Making Change

When you buy something and pay more than the price, you get change back!

How to find change: Subtract the price from what you paid.
Example: Item costs $3.50, you pay $5.00 → Change = $5.00 - $3.50 = $1.50

Count-up method: Start at the price and count up to what you paid!
$3.50 → $3.75 (quarter) → $4.00 (quarter) → $5.00 (dollar) = $1.50 change
