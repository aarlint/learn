🔗 What Are Compound Words?

Compound words are made by putting two smaller words together to make a new word!

sun + flower
= sunflower 🌻
rain + bow
= rainbow 🌈
bed + room
= bedroom 🛏️
foot + ball
= football 🏈

👂 What Are Homophones?

Homophones are words that sound the same but have different meanings and different spellings!

there / their / they're
place / belonging / they are
to / too / two
direction / also / number 2
your / you're
belonging to you / you are
its / it's
belonging to it / it is

✏️ Spelling Patterns

English has patterns that help us spell! Learn these common rules:

i before e
believe, friend, field (except after c: receive, ceiling)
Silent e
Adding e makes the vowel say its name: hop→hope, kit→kite
Double consonant
Short vowel + consonant, double it: run→running, hop→hopping
Plurals
Most: add s. Words ending in s/sh/ch/x: add es (boxes, wishes)
